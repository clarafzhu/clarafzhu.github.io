About Us

We live in an era of fragmentation. At J.D. Phoebe Group, sustainability must evolve — sustaining our culture, intellect, rights, and the human spirit. We are not a traditional holding company; we are a holistic ecosystem where knowledge flows, legacy is secured across borders, culture is revitalized, and empathy is a force for good.

Our Vision

A borderless world where cultural heritage is honored, educational opportunity is universal, and mental resilience is a fundamental human right.

Our Mission

Orchestrate a symbiotic ecosystem of high-value services — law, education, culture, and trade — generating resources to fund the sustainability of the human spirit through the J.D. Phoebe Foundation.

The Golden Thread: Holistic Sustainability
Shi Xin sustains Civilization · Coursistant sustains Human Potential · J.D. Phoebe Law sustains Security & Legacy · The Foundation sustains Hope.
