About Us

J.D. Phoebe Law is an elite legal advisory firm dedicated to managing the complete lifecycle of global citizenship and international commerce. Headquartered with a strategic focus on the United States and cross-border jurisdictions, our team comprises world-class, Esq.-certified attorneys from diverse legal backgrounds, including the U.S. mainland and Hong Kong. We move beyond transactional law to provide sophisticated, bespoke solutions that ensure our clients have the freedom to move, the compliance to trade, and the security to stay.

Core Services

Immigration & Global Mobility: Tailored legal pathways for global citizens, executives, entrepreneurs, and exceptional talents. We seamlessly manage the complexities of U.S. immigration and cross-border relocations for both corporate entities and high-net-worth individuals.

Cross-Border Trade Compliance: Safeguarding your international commerce. We guide multinational corporations through the intricate web of international trade regulations, customs, tariffs, and cross-border commercial law, ensuring frictionless global operations.

Private Client & Asset Protection: Comprehensive legacy planning. We build robust legal frameworks for cross-border asset protection, family trusts, and corporate structuring, ensuring your wealth and legacy are secured across international borders.

Why Us

We integrate mobility, trade compliance, and private client strategy into a single, coherent plan across jurisdictions — anticipating risks before they become liabilities. With a cross-border lens and a bespoke approach, we help clients move with confidence, trade with clarity, and preserve legacy with durable legal architecture.
